1. Vegetable Omelette

    Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs
  • Chopped onions, tomatoes, green pepper
  • Salt, black pepper, oil
    Procedure:
  1. Beat eggs in a bowl.
  2. Mix with chopped vegetables and seasoning.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, pour mixture and cook both sides.

2. Chicken Stir Fry

    Ingredients:
  • Chicken breast, soy sauce, garlic, vegetables, oil
    Procedure:
  1. Slice chicken and veggies.
  2. Stir-fry garlic and chicken until golden.
  3. Add veggies and soy sauce, cook for 5 mins.

3. Ugali & Sukuma Wiki

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups maize flour
  • Water, kale (sukuma), oil, onions, salt
    Procedure:
  1. Boil water, add flour gradually while stirring until thick.
  2. Cook kale with onions, oil, and salt.
  3. Serve together while hot.
